Marcus Aurelius

The Philosopher King

Here's a timeline of Marcus Aurelius's life:

  • 121 CE - Born in Rome, Italy
  • 138 CE - Adopted by Emperor Antoninus Pius, marking the beginning of his path to leadership
  • 161 CE - Becomes Roman Emperor alongside Lucius Verus
  • 167 CE - Faces the Marcomannic Wars, demonstrating his leadership and dedication to the empire
  • 170 CE - Begins writing "Meditations," his philosophical reflections and guide to self-improvement
  • 175 CE - Faces internal rebellion but successfully restores stability to the empire
  • 180 CE - Dies in Vindobona (modern-day Vienna), leaving behind a legacy as a philosopher and ruler

"You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength."

-- Marcus Aurelius, from Meditations
